Most Common (National)

#1 The Most Common: this is the penultimate position in the club that manages and control the entire membership of the institution. It’s a position with a term limit of one year or sometimes extended to some few months based on the circumstances on campus. However once you attained this title you’ll always hold it but loose the powers and become an ex-Most common but you maintain your respect (Once a Most always a Most). The present Most Common holds the supreme executive authority.

Founder Idrissa Salam Conteh

Atomic Pen

#2 The Commoners Club started as a students’ social club for less privileged students who could not integrate into the existing social structures on the Fourah Bay College Campus. The Club’s pioneer and founder, Idrissa Salam CONTEH, felt disappointed at the attitude of students on campus whom he says attributed students’ social activities to students’ social background, family relations and material values.

    To be a Commoner

    You must be a registered student in a tertiary institution. We need evidence that shows that you are a registered student of the institution (e.g. copies of your acceptance letter, etc.) For continuing students, must have above Average grade point We need to see a copy of result or give evidence of your previous exams like your progress result or transcript Must be a member of the Black family/house on campus An executive member or active member must confirm that you are a member of the black family (The House) either from your previous institution or within your current institution. Purchase of club form
